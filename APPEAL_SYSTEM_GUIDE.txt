5. You receive DM notification when decision is made

**Restrictions:**
â€¢ Cannot appeal someone else's cases (unless you're admin)
â€¢ Cannot re-appeal already decided cases (unless previously denied)
â€¢ Must wait 24 hours between appeals
â€¢ Case must be active or appealable

different administrators. This ensures serious repeat offenders receive thorough
review before case removal.
